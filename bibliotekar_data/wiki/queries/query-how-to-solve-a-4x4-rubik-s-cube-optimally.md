# God’s Number 4×4

**Concept:** God’s Number for the 4×4×4 Rubik’s Cube (Rubik’s Revenge)

Current research bounds God’s number (the minimum number of moves required to solve the cube in the worst case) between 35 and 55 moves in the outer block turn metric, between 32 and 53 moves in the single slice turn metric, and between 29 and 53 moves in the block turn metric. Probabilistic estimates published around 2024–2025 conjecture optimal values of 48 quarter‑turns or 41 half‑turns.【803414087416734†L364-L367】【803414087416734†L425-L434】

# Query: What is the God’s number for the 4×4 cube?

God's number for the 4×4 cube (Rubik's Revenge) remains unknown. Current research places it between 35 and 55 moves in the outer block turn metric, between 32 and 53 moves in the single slice turn metric, and between 29 and 53 moves in the block turn metric. Recent conjectures suggest an optimal quarter‑turn metric of 48 moves and a half‑turn metric of 41 moves.


# Query: God’s Number 4×4

The exact God’s number for the 4×4×4 Rubik’s Cube is unknown.  Current best‑known bounds place it between 35 and 55 moves in the outer block turn metric, 32–53 moves in the single slice turn metric, and 29–53 moves in the block turn metric【803414087416734†L364-L367】.  Probabilistic analyses suggest that the optimum may be 48 quarter‑turns or 41 half‑turns【803414087416734†L425-L434】. **Concept:** God’s Number for the 4×4×4 Rubik’s Cube (Rubik’s Revenge)

Current research bounds God’s number (the minimum number of moves required to solve the cube in the worst case) between 35 and 55 moves in the outer block turn metric, between 32 and 53 moves in the single slice turn metric, and between 29 and 53 moves in the block turn metric. Probabilistic estimates published around 2024–2025 conjecture optimal values of 48 quarter‑turns or 41 half‑turns.【803414087416734†L364-L367】【803414087416734†L425-L434】
