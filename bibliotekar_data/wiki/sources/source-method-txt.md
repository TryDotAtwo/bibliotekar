To solve a 4x4 Rubik's cube optimally, you typically use the reduction method. First solve all the center pieces on each face. Next, pair the edge pieces into dedges. Finally, solve the cube as a 3x3 using your favourite 3x3 algorithms. Watch out for parity errors that occur on 4x4 cubes, which require special algorithms to fix.
